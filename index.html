KUCCPS Application Guide

KUCCPS places Form 4 leavers into government-sponsored programmes. Applications open February–April each year at kuccps.net

1
Await KCSE results from KNEC (released Jan/Feb)
2
Register at kuccps.net with your KCSE index number
3
Select up to 3 course choices in order of priority
4
Pay KES 2,057 application fee via M-Pesa
5
Await placement — revision window opens for rejected applicants

Funding & Scholarships

Everything Kenyan students need to know about paying for university — from HELB loans to government bursaries and scholarships.

🎓

HELB – Higher Education Loans Board

HELB is Kenya's primary higher education funding body, providing loans and bursaries to eligible Kenyan students. Applications open annually in September. Visit helb.co.ke to apply.

1
Register at helb.co.ke with your national ID
2
Fill in the online loan application form
3
Upload: ID, admission letter, KRA PIN, guardian details
4
Await review and approval (4–6 weeks)
5
Funds disbursed to university + personal M-Pesa account
6
Repay loan 2 years after graduation (salary deductions)
💵
HELB Loan Amounts
Govt Sponsored (min)KES 35,000/yr
Govt Sponsored (max)KES 60,000/yr
Self-Sponsored (Module II)Up to KES 150,000/yr
Bursary (needy students)KES 8,000–30,000/yr

📋
Eligibility Checklist
☑️ Must be a Kenyan citizen
☑️ Valid KCSE certificate
☑️ Admission letter from accredited university
☑️ Valid KRA PIN (or parent's PIN)
☑️ No prior HELB benefit at same level
☑️ Submit before HELB deadline (September)
💡
Pro Tips for Maximum Funding
🏠

Apply for County Bursaries

Every Kenyan county allocates bursaries through the ward fund. Visit your county government offices in July–August. Amounts range from KES 5,000 to 80,000.

📅

Don't Miss HELB Deadline

HELB applications close in September each year. Missing it means waiting a full year. Register early at helb.co.ke as soon as you receive your admission letter.

🎯

Stack Multiple Sources

Combine HELB + county bursary + church/mosque bursary + employer sponsorship. Many students fund their education through multiple simultaneous sources.

📞

HELB Contact & Support

Call HELB: 0800 724 352 (toll-free) or email info@helb.co.ke. Open Mon–Fri, 8am–5pm EAT. Offices at Upper Hill, Nairobi.
